## Decisions Made

- **save_top_k=-1 over save_top_k=1**: Per RESEARCH Pitfall 3, `save_top_k=1` without `monitor=` causes Lightning `MisconfigurationException`. `save_top_k=-1` saves every epoch with no monitor required, matching tutorial-scale use.
- **ModelCheckpoint as first element**: Prepended (not appended) to callbacks list so it is always present regardless of `cfg.eval.knn` state.
- **TDD gate compliance**: Two separate RED/GREEN cycles — one for `train.py` (B3) and one for `README.md` (B5 doc).
